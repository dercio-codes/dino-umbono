About Us

Umbono Eco-Developments (UED) mission is to promote skills development and innovation by providing our customers with the highest level of quality construction, home improvements and building maintenance that would lead to an improved quality of life to communities within South Africa. We aim to ensure the highest levels of professionalism, integrity, honesty and fairness in our relationships with our suppliers, subcontractors, professional associates and customers, whilst upskilling youth from disadvantaged communities. Our mission is to provide our employees with an honest and helpful working environment, where every employee individually and collectively, can dedicate themselves to providing our customers with exceptional workmanship, extraordinary service, and professional integrity.

Mission

UED envisions a world dedicated to sustainable developments, with the use of sustainable materials, methods and relationships. The idea is to ensure the betterment of our community and the environment within through teachings and innovation dedicated to the services we provide. Though not always cost effective, the information gathered can be researched to develop alternative sustainable methods for future use.

Values

Umbono Eco-Developments values Honesty, Integrity and most of all, Professionalism . We aim to keep our word and provide only the highest quality of service for our clients. Umbono values its community and understands that successful business are made by entire workforces and not management alone. We strive to ensure a diverse working environment, filled with learning and innovative problem solutions that work to benefit the environment and its communities.
